Philosophy

Data, Systems, and Execution

I work at the seam where operations, finance, and technology collide. My job is to make systems reliable, metrics defensible, and new capabilities safe to ship. That includes modern analytics, automation, and AI, but the priority is always the same: business outcomes you can stand behind.

Strategy

Systems win when they are designed around decisions. I map KPIs to workflows, define metrics once, and build reporting and automation that stays correct as the business changes.

Scale

I modernize legacy environments without breaking the business. That means staged migrations, governed datasets, quality gates, and interfaces that let domain teams move faster with fewer surprises.

Impact

I tie engineering to outcomes: cycle-time reduction, cost removal, and fewer manual steps. The goal is a business that can answer questions fast, act confidently, and audit how decisions were made.
